What to do when you're finished Cleaning

​

When you go to a client's home to clean, tap on the bubbles icon (first icon on the left) to see a quick view of the cleaning tasks for that room

​

Hit the send button to send the completed tasks to your client

​

Your client should receive a text message containing a link to your completed tasks

​

Your client can now open the link to view the completed tasks, price, and leave you a feedback
