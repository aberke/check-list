NeatStreak Menu

​

Once you're in the dashboard, tap the icon in the upper right corner to open the menu

​

The Menu contains your name on top. 

You can tap sign out to log out of NeatStreak

Tap "Español" to switch the language to Spanish

​

Once the language is switched to Spanish, tap the icon to continue.

Or, tap "English" to switch back to English
