NeatStreak lets house cleaners create clear cleaning agreements with their clients.

How It Works

NeatStreak is a mobile website which lets house cleaners create and implement clear cleaning agreements with their clients.

Feedback

After each cleaning appointment, the cleaner sends a cleaning receipt to the client. The client can then give feedback on today’s cleaning tasks and request changes for the next appointment.

Cleaning Agreements

Cleaner and client use NeatStreak to select tasks from a cleaning menu, define custom tasks, note how particular tasks should be done and record what the entire cleaning job will cost. The cleaner then sends a copy of the new cleaning agreement to the client.

Bi-lingual Communication

Spanish-speaking cleaners can use NeatStreak in their native language while clients receive all communications in English or vice versa.

How it Looks

Home Page

From the home page a cleaner can create a new account or sign in. They can also switch the entire interface to Spanish.

Dashboard

On the dashboard, a cleaner sees a list of the cleaning agreements for all their current clients. From here cleaners can modify an agreement or go to a quick view of the cleaning tasks for the home they are cleaning today.

Cleaning Agreement

From this view cleaners can modify a cleaning agreement for a particular client and share the new agreement with the client.
